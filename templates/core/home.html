Our Story

We started our martabak journey with a simple goal: to create the most delicious martabak in town. Using traditional recipes passed down through generations, we've perfected our craft to bring you authentic flavors.

Our menu features three signature martabak varieties:

  • Martabak Telur - Savory egg martabak with minced meat and spices
  • Martabak Manis - Sweet martabak with various toppings
  • Martabak Tipis Kering - Thin and crispy martabak
